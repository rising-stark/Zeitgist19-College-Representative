About Us

Zeitgeist is one of the biggest annual college's cultural festival of North India: A fiesta where art, talent, and diversity meets. It has a total footfall of more than 12k & a reach of more than 250 colleges. Encapsulating the spirit of times, Zeitgeist provides an opportunity to the people from all over India to showcase their talent. It provides a platform to the young talents of our nation to stir the atmosphere, rock the audience and set the stage on fire. Zeitgeist has hosted many talented and celebrated personalities like Vishal Shekhar, Mohit Chauhan, Alfaz, Zephyrtone, DJ Olly Esse and so on. Filled with the exhilaration of heated competition and exuberant events, Zeitgeist'19 is here to rock your heart.

FAQ

  • Who are College Representatives?
    CAs are liaisons b/w Zeigeist & their college. They're active leaders in their campus community who act as representative of Zeitgeist.

  • How to apply for CA?
    By clicking on 'REGISTER' button on the CA Portal at official Zeitgeist website.

  • What is the eligibility to become a CA?
    College student having valid College ID Card.

  • How many CAs can be there from a college?
    If student participation from one college is large then, more than one CA can be appointed.

  • How much time and effort would be expected from me?
    Completion of assigned task with dedication & seriousness.
